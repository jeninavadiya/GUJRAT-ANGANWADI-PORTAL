About us

Anganwadi is a type of rural child care centre in India. They were started by the Indian government in 1975 as part of the Integrated ChildDevelopment Services program to combat child hunger and malnutrition.



In Anganwadi, after more than 3 decades of integrated child development service (ICDS) implementation, the process of demanding resources from higher authorities and the records of inventory details are in the form of paper work, which still remains a matter of concern. So the process is time-consuming ,hard to manage and track the records for all beneficiaries.



To ovecome the above problem, we intend to make a web portal that connects the Anganwadi offices and the higher authorities ,in which the Anganwadi workers can generate the demand for their required resources online and have all the inventory provided to them.
